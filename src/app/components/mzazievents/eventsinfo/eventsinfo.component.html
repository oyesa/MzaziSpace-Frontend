About the event

Summer Dinner brings single parents together to help them reach their fullest potential and inspire the rest of the world and their communities.

What is Summer Dinner?

Summer Dinner is a community organization that was started in 2010. The community currently has over reach of over 1000 members spread across East Africa, made of single parents and counselors. In terms of development the community focuses on supporting and empowering single parents.

The community hosts weekly and monthly meetups in different cities in East Africa. The community brings you Summer Dinner event every year with different themes to fill the gap that exist and in return helps spur innovation and growth in contribution to global economy.
