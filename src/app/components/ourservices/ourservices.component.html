OUR SERVICES

As the Mzazi Space community has grown significantly over the years, it has become clear through feedback
from the community that single parents often find traditional service methods insufficient.With this is mind, we decided to create a respectful and empowering community experience for single parents where
everyone has the same shared importance of family with Mzazi Space. Already many parents have
found services offered to their proximity, and we are excited to see many
more lovely and blended families join us in the future.


Find a Counselor

Childcare Services

Mzazi Space also offers daycare services as well as therapy for the young ones. If your child needs some help you can reach out to us.
