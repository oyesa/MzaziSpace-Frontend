Who we are

Mzazi Space is a single parent App that aims to match single parents with similar interests so they can help each other along the journey of single parenthood. Mzazi Space is a community for all single parents.We know it can get hard, We got you .

Our History

Mzazi Space was founded on 28th June 2022 by a group of wonderful people who call themselves 'The Morning Brewers'. We know that there can be highs and lows to being a single parent. So at Mzazi Space we offer ways to connect to other single parents.
