Child Services

Childcare is important for both children’s development and parents’ employment. For single parents, childcare is particularly important to help make work possible.
As both the main carer and main earner, single parents can’t ‘shift-parent’ in the same way couple parents do in order to
manage nursery and school pick-ups and drop-offs.
Single parents tell us that the lack of affordable and flexible childcare available locally
often prevents them from entering (or re-entering) work.
